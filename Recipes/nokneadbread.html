No Knead Bread

Seriously! No kneading necessary.

The hardest part of this recipe is to remember to mix it up the night before you want to eat the bread.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups bread flour
  • 1/2 t yeast
  • 2 t salt
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl just until all flour is wet. I like to use a butter knife to mix the ingredients.
  2. Cover and let sit on the counter for 8-12 hours. The dough should expand to twice the size and be really wet.
  3. Preheat oven and dutch oven to 475°F.
  4. Pour some olive oil on the counter (and your hands) and pour dough out onto the counter. Work dough just enough to cover it with the olive oil.
  5. Place dough in dutch oven and bake, covered, for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove lid from dutch oven and lower the temperature to 425°F and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
  7. Remove from dutch oven and let cool 15 minutes before slicing.
