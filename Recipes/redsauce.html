Red Sauce

  • 1/2 to 3/4 cup olive oil (get the best you can afford)
  • 3-4 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 - 2 teaspoons basil
  • 1 large can tomato sauce (29 oz)

Place olive oil and garlic in a medium sized sauce pan. Heat until garlic is golden (but don't burn it!). Add tomato sauce, red pepper, and basil. Simmer for as long as you can (minimum one hour).

Optional Items:

  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 egg (just crack it and stir it in)
  • 1 beef bullion cube

Yield: Enough sauce for a pound of pasta.
 or
Yield: Enough sauce for 8 calzone.
Source: Italy Rome Mission, 1987-1989.
