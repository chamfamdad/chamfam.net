Pumpkin Cookies

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange peel
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts

Mix sugar, pumpkin, shortening, and orange peel. Stir in dry ingredients. Stir in nuts and raisins. Drop by teaspoon onto greased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes at 375 degrees. Remove immediately from sheet and cool. Spread with brown glaze.

Light Brown Glaze

  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons milk

Melt margarine over medium heat until delicate brown. Stir in powdered sugar. Add vanilla and milk (one tablespoon at a time) until smooth.

Yield: about 3 dozen
Source: Kathy Hatch's Relief Society's Favorite Pumpkin Recipes
