Cranberry Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 package onion soup mix
  • 1 can cranberry sauce (with or without berries)
  • 1 bottle catalina or french salad dressing
  • 3 lbs chicken

Instructions

  1. Stir onion soup mix, cranberry sauce, and salad dressing together.
  2. Place chicken in a large baking dish and pour other ingredients on top.
  3. Bake at 350 for about an hour, or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.

Yield: About 8 servings
