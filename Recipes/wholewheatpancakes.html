Whole Wheat Pancakes

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup whole wheat

Pour into blender. Grind on high for 3 to 4 minutes. Reduce to medium speed.

Add:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder

Scrape sides of blender and continue blending for 2 minutes. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto preheated griddle, turning when bubbles form and break in middle of pancake.

Yield: about 1 dozen small pancakes
Source: Aunt Martha
