No Bake Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 c sugar
  • 1/4 c cocoa
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/2 c milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 c peanut butter
  • 3 c oatmeal
  • 1 bag butterscotch chips

Instructions

  1. Put sugar and cocoa into a sauce pan and stir together.
  2. Add butter and milk, and heat mixture on stovetop over medium heat.
  3. Bring to a rolling boil and boil for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in vanilla and peanut butter and remove from heat.
  5. Stir in oatmeal and butterscotch chips.
  6. Scoop tablespoon size balls and place on wax paper to cool.
