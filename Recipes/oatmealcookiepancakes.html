Oatmeal Cookie Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 cup applesauce
  • 1 cup ripe bananas
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk

Instructions

  1. Stir dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Add wet ingredients and stir well.
  3. Cook on a hot griddle.

Yield: 8 pancakes, 8 inches in diameter.
Source: Fletcher Chambers
