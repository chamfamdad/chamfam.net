Ramen Salad

  • 1 bag coleslaw
  • diced green onions
  • 1 pack slivered almonds
  • sunflower seeds
  • 2 packages of beef Ramen

Remove the flavor packets from the Ramen and set aside. Crumble the Ramen noodles and mix with the other ingredients in a large salad bowl.

Dressing

  • 2 beef flavor packets set aside earlier
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil

Stir ingredients together in a small bowl. Mix with salad just before serving (to keep the noodles from getting soggy).
