Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 Tablespoon yeast (or one package)
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 3 Tablespoons vegetable oil
  • enough flour to make the dough not sticky, could be another 2 to 3 cups.

Instructions

  1. Place flour, yeast, sugar, and salt into a mixing bowl.
  2. Slowly add water and oil and mix.
  3. Continuing adding flour until the dough is manageable.
  4. Spread on a buttered cookie sheet.

Yield: 2 cookie sheet sized pizza doughs
