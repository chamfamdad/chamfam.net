Waffles

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Sift flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar. Add egg yolks and milk and beat to make a smotth batter. Stir in the melted butter, and at the last minute, fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Bake in hot, well greased waffle iron.

Yield: about 4 waffles
