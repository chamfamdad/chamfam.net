Mississippi Mud Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/3 cup cocoa
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cup flour
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cream together butter, sugar, and cocoa.
  2. Add eggs one at a time beating well after each egg.
  3. Add flour, vanilla, and walnuts to the mixture.
  4. Bake in greased 9 x 13 pan at 350 for 25 minutes.
  5. Add 1/2 pound miniature marshmallows on top. Return to oven for 3 minutes.
  6. Then spread marshmallows.
  7. Cool for 1 hour.
  8. Add frosting.

Frosting

  • 1 pound powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup softened butter
  • 1/4 cup cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg plus enough water to make 1/3 cup

Instructions

  1. Beat all ingredients together.

Yield: about 24 brownies
