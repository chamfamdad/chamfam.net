Unified Declarations

The board has been working with the Association’s attorneys, Kaman & Cusimano, to assist with drafting a set of unified Declarations. The unified Declarations will:

  • Make the Declarations for all of the phases in the neighborhood as uniform as possible and will provide continuity between the phases
  • Help preserve property values in a single, streamlined set of documents that govern the Association
  • Eliminates unnecessary references in the Declarations
  • Restricts County Sheriff-notified Tier III and Tier II sex offenders from being able to reside in our community
  • Be consistent with Ohio’s “Planned Community” laws

Homeowners should have received a packet of information about this effort. Items included in the packet were:

  • Letter of explanation and instructions for voting (49kb PDF)
  • Self addressed stamped envelope
  • Voting form (37kb PDF)
  • Unified Declarations (105kb PDF)
