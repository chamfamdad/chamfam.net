Sunday, July 20, 2003

In the morning we got ready and went to church. But we arrived too late. It was a single's ward and they did not have any Primary. We would have to come back at 1:00 pm for Sacrament meeting only because they could not handle any extra Primary kids. So we went down to the visitor's center in Nauvoo and waited for it to open.

Some of the places we visited in Nauvoo were the brick making, the Webb Blacksmith Shop, the Scovil Bakery, and the Jonathan Browing Home and Gun Shop.

About twenty miles from Nauvoo is Carthage, Illinois. We also went there to see Carthage Jail.
