Monday, July 28, 2003

Today we plan to visit Mesa Verde and Four Corners Monument.

It is not an easy thing to gather such a large group and take an outing. We were supposed to meet Eric and Lydia at their hotel at 10:00 am and then drive together to Mesa Verde. But in an effort to get Bruce and Kelsey to go, we ended up waiting for them at Eric and Lydia's hotel. However, Eric and Lydia wanted to meet up with one of Lydia's friends on our way to the park. So we ended up staying at Eric and Lydia's hotel to wait for Bruce and Kelsey, while Eric and Lydia went off to meet up with their friends. I believe it was 12:30 pm before we ever all got to Mesa Verde.

After a brief visit at just one of the sites. We found a place to have a picnic lunch.

After lunch (I think it was about 3:30 pm), we all went our separate ways. Eric and Lydia went back to their hotel and Grandma and Grandpa Hatch came with us to visit the Four Corners Monument before returning to Bloomfield.
