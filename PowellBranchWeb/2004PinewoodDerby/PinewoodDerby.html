Pinewood Derby 2004

Cub Scout Pack 132

Thursday, March 25, 2003
The Cub Scouts held their annual pinewood derby tonight. 11 boys participated in the event. They were split into one group of 6 cars and one group of 5 cars. The top three cars in each group raced in the finals.
Trophies were presented to the top three finishers and were as follows:
  • First: Ryan Rupa
  • Second: Blake Simmons
  • Third: David McNamara
