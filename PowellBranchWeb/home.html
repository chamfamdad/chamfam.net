Powell Branch

Welcome to the unofficial site of the Powell Branch of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. (For the official site, please click here.)

Mostly I wanted to document the construction progress of our new chapel. Some of you do not get the chance to drive by the building everyday, so I thought I could take a picture every day and comment on the progress that I notice.

Important Notice (Updated 18 July 2003, 6:05 pm)

This just in:
Church will be at Powell at 9:30am Sunday July 20th. We will definitely need to call everyone as this has been back and forth and at the last minute Powell City pushed this through and we have the occupancy permit. I never doubted for a minute!

We are really sorry for any confusion, but would like to do as President Flannery said and call all of our families and get the word out to everyone. Please call if you have questions.

-Kevin

This domain name (powellbranch.org) is set to expire on August 1, 2003. Since I only created this site for the purpose of documenting the progress of the building I have decided not to renew the name. For official news of the Powell Branch please see the official site of the Powell Branch.
