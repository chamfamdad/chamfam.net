Pinewood Derby

Cub Scout Pack 132

Tuesday, March 25, 2003
The Cub Scouts held their annual pinewood derby tonight. 10 boys participated in the event. They were split into two groups of 5 and participated in a round-robin tournament.
The top two cars from each group then moved on to the finals. The finals were also held in round-robin fashion.
Trophies were presented to the top four places and were as follows:
  • First: A.J.
  • Second: Spencer
  • Third: Ryan
  • Fourth: David
A Trophy was also presented to the car that represented the "Best of Show". That car was judged to be David's (#12 in the picture above).
All boys who participated received a display stand for their car.
