Thursday, October 31, 2002

I don't have a clue what went on today.

Wednesday, October 30, 2002

Most of the underlayment on the front wall was put up today. That means that most of the outside walls of the entire building has underlayment.
Home

Tuesday, October 29, 2002

There wasn't much done today in the rain.
Home

Monday, October 28, 2002

Sorry I didn't get a photo last Friday. It rained most of the day so I wasn't able to get over there. Before the rain began, the workers were able to place a few boards on the front wall of the chapel.

Today, I didn't notice much of a difference from Friday. I did see the workers there working, but I could not tell what work was compeleted.

Home

Thursday, October 24, 2002

They continued working on the rafters today. Most of the north side of the building was completed. Now I think they will begin work on the dormers.

Something funny I noticed today is that from a distance, the rafters look like a stealth bomber. The top picture on the right demonstrates this effect the best.

Home

23 October 2002

They continued working on the rafters today.

They were also spraying the rafters today. I am not sure if they were washing them, or treating them with something. Though, from the smell in the area, I am guessing it was insecticide.

Home

22 October 2002

They continued working on the rafters today.
Home

21 October 2002

Wow, there were some noticable improvements today! They started on the roof.

They also added some framing for the entry way on either side of the building.

Home

19 October 2002

Yesterday some of the roof trusses showed up
Home

17 October 2002

Again today they worked on ceilings in the classrooms and hallways.
Home

16 October 2002

Today I saw them working on the ceilings in the class rooms and the hallways. By doing this they are helping to support the walls in the chapel. Then, when the chapel walls are strong enough, they can start the roof trusses (though we could still be a week or more away on that).
Home

15 October 2002

There was definitely work done today. The tall walls on the sides of the chapel were put up. They also spread gravel around the parking lot in preparation for the asphalt.
Home

14 October 2002

It was hard to tell if there was any work done today. Maybe they worked on the parking lot. It seemed a little smoother. Or maybe they had Columbus Day off?
Home

11 October 2002

I did not see any changes today from yesterday.
Home

10 October 2002

Today they starting covering the outside walls. This will strengthen them so thay can put the rafters on (though we are still a long way away from being ready for the rafters).

I also noticed some grading in the parking lot.

Home

9 October 2002

And the walls come down!?

Today they took down the walls in the chapel portion of the building. I don't know why, but they were gone today.
