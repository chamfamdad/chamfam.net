Friday, February 28, 2003

I saw some activity on the roof, but I didn't see that anything got finished. There are still some edges on the roof that need to be completed.
Home

Thursday, February 27, 2003

I've seen a lot of drywall scraps coming out of the building. I imagine the walls are really taking shape.
Home

Wednesday, February 26, 2003

I've seen a lot of drywall going into the building. I imagine the walls are starting to take shape.
There was also someone there surveying some points around the building.
Home

Tuesday, February 25, 2003

Today the northwest corner of the building is exposed and the bricks look very nice. There is also a host of people inside working.
Home

Monday, February 24, 2003

I think I saw the bricklayers working today. It's hard to tell because they are underneath the plastic.
Home

Thursday, February 20, 2003

It looks like they are finally going to run the permanent electric line. There was a backhoe there digging a hole on both sides of the road.
Home

Tuesday, February 18, 2003

There were quite a few more workers there today. I guess the roads are finally getting cleared off.
Home

Monday, February 17, 2003

There wasn't much activity today. I guess everyone was snowed in!
Home

Friday, February 14, 2003

The bricklayers set up again around the southwest corner of the building. They had set up there once before but moved to the north entry way before they actually did any work on that corner.
Home

Thursday, February 13, 2003

I took pictures inside today. You can view them all here.
Home

Wednesday, February 12, 2003

They installed a few more windows.
Home

Tuesday, February 11, 2003

More windows were installed today.

Monday, February 10, 2003

Today I got a picture of some of the windows. I actually noticed them last Thursday, but was unable to get a photo until now.

There are a few other windows in place also.
