Wednesday, April 30, 2003

They worked on the stucco around the south entrance today. I did not get a good look at the north entrance.
Home

Tuesday, April 29, 2003

They continued to carve out the parking lot today. I also noticed the pole for the sattelite dish in the back of the parking lot.
Home

Monday, April 28, 2003

They moved a lot of dirt today. It looks like they are starting to shape the parking lot. I haven't noticed any work on the sidewalks for a few days.
Home

Friday, April 25, 2003

They worked on the windows and doors on the south entrance today. I have also noticed throughout the week that they have been staying late every night.
Home

Thursday, April 24, 2003

Work continued on the sidewalks and I also noticed a concrete pad back by the out building.

They also installed the windows and doors on the north entrance.

Home

Wednesday, April 23, 2003

Work continued on the sidewalks and also the shingles on the roof. It looks like the front face of the building is now complete.
Home

Tuesday, April 22, 2003

The front sidewalk was poured today. They were also on the roof finishing up the edges.

Monday, April 21, 2003

They worked on finishing up the front of the building today.
