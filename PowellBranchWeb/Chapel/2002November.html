Friday, November 29, 2002

Obviously there was no work done yesterday (and it's actually against the law to work on a holiday in Powell).

Today they began putting on the vapor barrier. They also had to redo some of the tar paper on the roof because the wind was blowing it off.

Home

Wednesday, November 27, 2002

They are still working on finishing the porch/entry ways, plus there is a flurry of activity inside the building.

Tuesday, November 26, 2002

They worked on finishing the porch/entry ways.
Home

Monday, November 25, 2002

Did our fire hydrant turn yellow?

The plumber was there doing work. And I did not notice any activity on the roof (although it was wet this morning).

Home

Thursday, November 22, 2002

There are now lights inside the building (although temporary). So somehow they got electricity inside. I saw the electric company working on a transformer down the street earlier this week. I guess that is what was needed for electricity.
Home

Thursday, November 21, 2002

Del-Co Water was there today installing the water tap.
Home

Wednesday, November 20, 2002

Work continued on the dormers and entry ways. It was a very nice day for working outside!
Home

Tuesday, November 19, 2002

They are still working on the entry ways and dormers.
Home

Monday, November 18, 2002

They worked on the rafters for the entry way on each side and the rafters for the dormers today.
Home

Friday, November 15, 2002

Today they continued on the roof, until it sprinkled, and then they all got down. You never want to be on a wet roof. I don't know if it will dry out enough to continue work or not.

They did finish the tar paper before having to get off.

Home

Thursday, November 14, 2002

They were able to complete the roof deck yesterday. And today they began with the tar paper. I was surprised to see them start on that without finishing the dormers first. I guess there is a lot of roof that you can shingle without getting in the way of the dormers.
Home

Wednesday, November 13, 2002

They continued with the roof decking today. I am guessing they should be able to complete it by the end of the day.

They also started the porch/entry way on the north side of the building.

Home

Tuesday, November 12, 2002

Sorry, too dark to take a picture today. They finished the roof deck on the north slope and started on the south slope.
Home

Monday, November 11, 2002

They began decking the roof today. The finished about half of the north slope.

They were also doing things inside the building, but now that the underlayment is up on the outside walls, I can no longer see what is going on inside.

Home

Friday, November 8, 2002

Because they did not shingle the out building, the tar paper has blown off. They will now have to redo the tar paper on the out building.
Home

Thursday, November 7, 2002

The outside of the building is taking shape. More underlayment was placed on the front of the building. But they still need to replace a few walls before the front will be done.
Home

Wednesday, November 6, 2002

I think they have been placing a studs inside the rafters over the past few days. And since it is inside, I can't see what they are doing.

Does the roof look crooked to you? Maybe the photo is just at the wrong angle? I don't know, but it looks crooked to me. Actually, maybe they just aren't finished with this side yet. The other side has a board across the face that would straighten it up a bit.

Home

Tuesday, November 5, 2002

Sorry, no picture today. I didn't want to get the camera wet. Even though it rained all day, the workers were there in the rain. They worked on the over hang on the roof (among other things I imagine).
Home

Monday, November 4, 2002

There were workers there working today. There were even workers there on Saturday (first time I have noticed them on Saturday). But they must have been working on things inside, because I could not tell what had been done.
Home

Friday, November 1, 2002

With the front wall on the church I can no longer tell what is going on inside. There didn't seem to be any work outside today.
