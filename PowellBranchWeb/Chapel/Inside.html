Thursday, January 30, 2003
Looking down the south hall toward the back of the building.

I received permission to go inside and take some photos today. From across the street the building seems small, but inside is a whole different story.

Standing in the south entry looking toward the center of the building. Standing in the gym looking toward the front of the chapel. The baptismal room. The font is covered by a platform.

Inside they have done an incredible amount of work. The plumbing looks complete (except for the fixtures which can't go in until the floor goes in). The heating and air conditioning is coming along. I noticed that the breaker box is completely empty, so there is still most of the electrical work to do. But I did see quite a few electrical boxes in place. The canned lighting fixtures were also hung in the chapel.

Standing in one of the large rooms in the back of the building. Closet in one of the large rooms in the back of the building. View of the north entry, from the chapel.

There is a minimal amount of drywall that has been hung. Mostly it has been hung in the attic area where many of the mechanicals are located. This tells me they are pretty much done with placing the mechanical equipment in the attic.
